The Best Movie According To David

My Top Movies of All Time:

Lord Of the Rings: The Return of the King

Awesome movie. I love the story and the characters. The special effects are amazing and the music is beautiful. I highly recommend this movie to everyone.

Lord Of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

This movie is also great. The world-building is incredible and the characters are well-developed. A must-watch for any fantasy lover.

Lord Of the Rings: The Two Towers

The second installment in the Lord of the Rings trilogy is just as good as the first.
